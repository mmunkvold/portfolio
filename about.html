And this is me...

"If I was young again I would be a programmer", I told a fresh-out-of high school young boy the summer of 2020...

And there you go - that was the start of doing something completely new* and something I really didn't think I would do as I didn't do it when I was STARTING my life way back in the 90's...

I'm so thrilled that I did it anyway, and now two years later, I am soon to be living my childhood dream, doing programming and web development.

My first encounter with the "programming" world was when I bought my first computer, an Amstrad CPC464, where I could change the color on the text by writing some code following a manual...

Now it sounds like I'm a computer nerd, with a room full of outdated hardware... I'm not. Really not. I can't even remember what happened to that Amstrad...

*In the years that followed I took graphic design, but didn't use it for work purposes. Later on I took some marketing and webdesign, but didn't use that for work purposes either. Then my brother and I started a tour operator company in Thailand and we needed a website for that. Finally I could get something out of what I have previously learnt.

Nowadays I try doing some small stuff in my spare time to get more experience, and I'm doing some freelancing making websites and internet ads on the side.

In my spare time I love to read, write, watch movies, go for a swim, do yoga and meditation, hug trees and cuddle up on the couch with my little buddy. And learn new stuff, I always want to learn new stuff.

And the magpie, you ask? Oh, I just love those rascals!
